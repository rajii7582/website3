Current AI solutions are built for very specialized and narrow applications. These programs are developed in silos and can’t collaborate in teams to solve complex problems, hindering broader adoption of AI solutions.

Most commercially available AI is focused on Machine Learning, a subfield of AI which is best suited to recognizing patterns but not understanding them.

As there is no understanding, Machine Learning can’t explain its thinking so can’t be given autonomy in complex life or death situations.

Machine Learning also requires massive amounts of data most people don’t have the resources to gather so for many applications it is not a practical option.
